🔥 Fire Fighting Bot

Smart Fire Detection & Suppression System

By Shravani Rajeev Bhosale

🚀 Introduction

A Fire Fighting Bot is an autonomous robot designed to detect and extinguish fire. It uses sensors to identify flames and activates a water pump to control fire, reducing risks to human life and property.

⚠️ Problem Statement

Fire accidents can cause severe damage and threaten human lives. Manual firefighting can be risky and delayed. This project introduces an automated robotic system for fast and safe fire suppression.

⚙️ Components Used

  • ESP32 Microcontroller
  • Flame Sensor
  • Servo Motors
  • Water Pump
  • Battery
  • Motor Driver

🤖 Working Principle

The flame sensor continuously detects fire presence. When fire is detected, the signal is sent to the ESP32 microcontroller. The robot moves toward the fire using servo motors and activates the water pump to extinguish the flame.

📡 Why ESP32?

ESP32 is a powerful microcontroller with built-in WiFi and Bluetooth. It enables faster processing and supports IoT-based fire alert systems, making the robot smarter and more efficient.

🌍 Applications

  • Smart homes
  • Industrial safety systems
  • Metro stations
  • Hazardous environments

🔮 Future Scope

  • IoT-based fire alert system
  • Camera integration
  • AI-based fire detection
